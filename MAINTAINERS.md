## Becoming a Maintainer

If you are an active contributor and would like to become a maintainer, please reach out through an issue.

**Criteria:**
- 3+ merged PRs
- Understanding of the project
- Commitment to ongoing participation

## Contact

- GitHub Discussions: General questions, idea sharing
